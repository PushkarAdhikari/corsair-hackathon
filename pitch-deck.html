The Problem

Email & Calendar are broken by design

1

You fight the inbox every day

Newsletters, promotions, and noise bury the emails that matter. Gmail's categories help — barely.

2

Calendar is a separate universe

Switching between email and calendar costs 23 seconds per context switch. You do it 40+ times a day.

3

Search is slow and shallow

Gmail search takes seconds. Cross-email-and-event search? Forget it. No offline. No vectors.

4

No AI-native workflow

Superhuman is fast, but it doesn't let you talk to your inbox. There's no agent that acts on your behalf.

😤

"I spend 4+ hours a day
on email and calendar"

The Solution

One command center. Zero friction.

1

AI-Priority Inbox

Every email is LLM-scored: Urgent, Focus, FYI, or Spam. Priority mail rises to the top automatically.

2

Unified Email + Calendar

One keystroke to switch. Calendar invites flow from emails. Events show email threads. No context switching.

3

Sub-second local search

All email and events cached in Postgres + pgvector. Search everything in under 1 second — offline-capable.

4

Agent Chat via Corsair MCP

"Send a calendar invite at 9 AM Thursday + an email" — one sentence. The agent drafts, schedules, and sends.

⚡

"Time spent on email cut by 60%"
